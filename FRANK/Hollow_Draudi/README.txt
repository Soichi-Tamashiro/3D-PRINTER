# Summary

Printed with the BCN3D Sigma R17, an independent dual extruder machine: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Uo9wGzFq-uI 

More awesome designs and prints: https://www.instagram.com/bcn3dtech/

The Hollow Draudi is designed to be created on resin 3D printers mainly due to their empty geometry. In addition, all orifices, modeled with Rhinoceros and Grasshopper, give it a unique personality and a plus of difficulty that will challenge any 3D printer FFF. 

Check out this cool timelapse printing the hollow draudi: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sWcapmmzpVg
