# Summary

■ design based on Flexy-Hand by Gyrobot.  
■ New [ gauntlet & Motor housing ] Design by U Hyeon Sung

■ FunMove redesign and modify to use Electronic Prosthetic Hand.  

■ PREPARATION  
1. 3D Printed FunMove Prosthetic Hand body  
2. 1 (Arduino nano) or (Arduino Pro - mini)  
3. 1 FSR(Pressure sensor) 
4. 1 Servo Motor (MG945 or MG946R)  
5. resister( 10K & 1K ) and capacitor ( 220uf )  
6. battery ( 5V ) or ( USB Port )  

FunMove_Arduino instruction : https://goo.gl/ImGJRc

● FunMove is a NPO in Korea.
We believe that human's the simplest happiness come from physical activity.
According to our belief, we are making prosthetic limb by utilizing Opensource hardware which are 3D printer and Arduino.

● You can see what we are doing from below.
- Developing 3D printed electronic prosthetic limb
- Donating 3D printed prosthetic limb to disabled
- Donating Opensource education to disabled so that they can build and modify their own prosthetic limb.
- Building up donation ecosystem. (Beneficiaries has to donate a prosthetic and opensource education to another disabled)
